About Us

The Literature & Law Project is a student-led nonprofit founded by a 10th-grade advocate for justice and literacy. We empower youth through legal education and the transformative power of books through two core programs: Books Behind Bars and MockTrial Minds.

Our Mission

We bridge the gap between legal education and literacy, empowering youth to advocate for themselves and others. We inspire confidence, compassion, and civic responsibility through books and courtroom experiences.
